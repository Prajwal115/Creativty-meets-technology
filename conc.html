Conclusion

Technology enhances creativity . It drastically reduces the cost of actualizing our intent . We can search domains , mix and match ideas and test concepts almost effortlessly . That means we can try out a lot more possibilities and increase tha chances of producing something truely outstanding .

Faliure in the analog world was expensive , tiresome and frustating , but in the digital world we can simulate faliure cheaply and easliy . From CAD software to 3d printers , A/B testing to agent based models , technology lets us experiment in the world of bits before we invest resources in the world of atoms .

As we become more technological society , we also become a more creative society , because many of the rote tasks that used to take up a plot of our time and effort have become automated . What's more , technology incresaes our potential to engage in types of experiences that lead to greater creativity .

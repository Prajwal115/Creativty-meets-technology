Interference of Technology over Creativity

Technology eradicates barriers to creative excellence

History is full of examples of missed opportunities because of deficiences of information . Clearly technology has transformed human experience . We are no longer separeted by time and space but are largely working off of the same massive database .

Mixing & Remixing

Knowledge is personal and information is a storable , fungible entity . It's become so easy to combine ideas through information that combining ideas no longer takes any particular skill . Personal expression , toil and labour reduced , process has become so completely automated .

Simulating Faliure

Most creative acts are faliures . Creative work is tightly linked to prolific output , the more work we produce , the more great work we produce . The problem is that it is often hard to separete good from the bad .

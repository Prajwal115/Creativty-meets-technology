Awesome Solutions - Expanding Possibilities

Key benefits of how technology has impacted creativity.

Innovation

Innovation happens by continously improving a process or product and technology has contributed in improving many of these. So Yes , technology is changing the phase of innovation.

Emerging Tools

It is important to highlight the different tools that have emerged through technology , such as 3d printing , digital content creation and other forms of digital interaction which give us the chance to explore new thing and to express ourselves in various ways.

Accessibility

Technology has enabled us to dream big and therefore it has fuelled creativity in many ways.There is a desire to invent and to create and with technology we can enter new avenues easily and connect with information and people around the world.

Forming Intent

Every creative act starts with a purpose wheteer it is a marketer , designer or an artist it is through forming intent that we esatablish the constrains under which creativity thrives .

Searching the Domain

All great artists or any body who is good at anything by throughly examining their domain they become aware of variety of techniques , alternative approcaches and different philosophies in all fields .

Tangling Hierarchies

Truely revolutionary creative acts come from synthesing across domains as innovations are largely the art of combinations of different insights .
